-Lab3Robot.cpp
Used to draw the Robot and control it.
1)w-forward
2)a-left
3)s-backwards
4)d-right
5)u-move light up
6)j-move light down
7)h-move light left
8)k-move light right
9)t-move light forward
10)g-move light backward
11)1-complete left hand and right leg
12)2-lower hand(left) and leg(right)
13)3-complete left leg and right hand
14)4-lower hand and leg(left)
15)-(minus)- 
16)+(plus)-
17)q-quit
18)'b' and ';' -control shine
